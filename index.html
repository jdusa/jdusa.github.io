Forgotten Battles

A topic I find interesting is often overlooked moments in history . One such moment is the involvement of the Czechoslovak Legion in the Russian Civil War.
This legion was able to capture significant portion of the Trans-Siberian Railway during their evacuation from Bolshevik Russia.

Mark on the World

The success of the Czechoslovak Legion became a contributing factor for the independence of Czechoslovakia because it caught the attention of Allied statesmen.
